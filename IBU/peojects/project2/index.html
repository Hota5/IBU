FROM HUMBLE BEGINNINGS TO TRUSTED LEADER

Founded in 2010 by travel enthusiasts John and Sarah Thompson, Road Runner Rentals started with just ten vehicles and a commitment to exceptional customer service. Through dedication and a focus on quality, the company quickly grew, expanding to multiple locations by 2015. Embracing technology, we launched an easy online booking system in 2016, further enhancing customer convenience. Today, we offer a wide range of reliable vehicles and remain steadfast in our mission to provide top-tier service and transparent pricing. Join us on the road and experience the Road Runner Rentals difference!
