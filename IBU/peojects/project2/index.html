Our Story

Founded in 2010 by travel enthusiasts John and Sarah Thompson, Road Runner Rentals began with a modest fleet of just ten vehicles and an unwavering commitment to exceptional customer service. From the very beginning, the Thompsons' dedication to quality and customer satisfaction set their business apart in the competitive car rental market.

Growth and Expansion

The company's focus on excellence quickly paid off. By 2015, Road Runner Rentals had expanded to multiple locations across Texas, providing more customers with reliable vehicles and outstanding service. This rapid growth was a testament to the company's dedication and hard work.

Embracing Technology

Recognizing the importance of technology in enhancing the customer experience, Road Runner Rentals launched an easy-to-use online booking system in 2016. This innovation allowed customers to reserve vehicles effortlessly from the comfort of their homes, adding a new level of convenience to their rental experience.

Our Commitment Today

Today, Road Runner Rentals offers a wide range of reliable vehicles to suit every need, from compact cars for city driving to spacious SUVs for family road trips. Despite our growth, we remain steadfast in our mission to provide top-tier service and transparent pricing. We believe in making the rental process as smooth and enjoyable as possible, ensuring that every journey with us is a pleasant one.

Join us on the road and experience the Road Runner Rentals difference! Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, our team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect vehicle for your needs, all while delivering the outstanding service that has become our hallmark.
