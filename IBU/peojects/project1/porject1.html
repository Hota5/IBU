The RoadRuner Story

Founded in 2001 amidst the vast landscapes of Texas, RoadRuner Rentals began with a simple mission: to redefine the rental experience. Jack Thompson and Sarah Ramirez, driven by their passion for exploration, envisioned a company that mirrored the speed and reliability of the legendary Texan roadrunners.

Reliability and affordability

With a commitment to reliability and affordability, we quickly expanded across the state. Today, with over two decades of experience, RoadRuner Rentals remains dedicated to delivering seamless journeys.
